Welcome to Gard.en!

Gard.en is a one-stop shop for people interested in patio/balcony gardening. Our content is suitable for everyone, from complete beginners who have never heard of patio gardening, to experienced gardeners who already have their own garden fully set up and running.

Patio gardening is a style of gardening suitable for a modern household - you no longer need a large back yard, they can be set up on the balcony of your apartment instead!


Growing your own vegetables is a fantastic way to save money while eating healthier, and it's fun too! Join the patio gardening community today with Gard.en. We have everything you need - information on how to get started and keep your garden running efficiently, a huge database of tips, tricks, and advice for every common plant and even a simple, drag-and-drop interactive app to help you plan your own garden!
